Context

As a long-established gas equipment supplier with a large customer base, the company's sales staff frequently have to go out to meet customers directly for consultation and support. At the same time, they need to continuously follow requests from various customers and quickly create quotes as needed. However, having to log into the company's complex system via a computer each time to perform these tasks is not only time-consuming but also challenging, especially when they are outside for work.

Goal

The application developed for tablets and mobile devices provides an optimal solution, allowing sales staff to easily create and manage quotes anywhere, without using computer. This application helps employees save time and quickly respond to customer requests, while also improving work efficiency and enhancing the customer experience.

Define Problems

Journey Map

The quoting feature already existed in the old CRM system; however, the system was too large and this flow was not clearly separated from other management features, we created a Journey Map to visualize the workflow of the sales staff. This helps all stakeholders gain a clearer and more comprehensive understanding of the entire process.

Solution

Create Quote

Creating quotes is the primary function of the app.
The process for create quotes from customer requests allows sales staff to easily use existing templates or build upon previous quotes to save time.

Quote management

Quotes are categorized so that sales staff can track their status and develop appropriate customer care plans

Customer management

Search for and track customer information

Reflection

1. Understanding the priorities of the direct user is crucial.

The old CRM system was designed to manage the entire enterprise, with its primary users being management-level employees. In contrast, this application is aimed at sales staff, so we need to understand the priorities of the main users. Management-level employees focus on overall views and strategic analysis, with robust reporting and management features, while sales staff prioritize functions that help them quickly and efficiently create and manage quotes, with an emphasis on detailed customer and product information.

2. Collaborating with other designers on the same Sketch file involved some challenges.

At the time of this project, Sketch did not have version management or real-time collaboration features, so we had to find a way to divide the work and establish a consistent process for updating the design files to avoid conflicts. Each designer would work on separate screens and then upload the files to Google Drive, after which we would consolidate the screens into a single file.
